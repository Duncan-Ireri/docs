lockers. You can integrate with the partner API, embed the checkout widget, or
connect an ecommerce platform.

## What UrbnBox handles

UrbnBox assigns available origin and destination lockers, calculates logistics
fees, generates deposit and collection credentials, and provides public
shipment tracking.

Your platform remains responsible for checkout, customer payment, and deciding
when to create a shipment.
